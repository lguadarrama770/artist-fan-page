Biography

Works/Accomplishments

Maluma has been nominated for many awards including:

  • MTV Europe Music Award for Best Latin America Central Act at the 2013 MTV EMAs
  • The Latin Grammy Award for Best New Artist
  • A win for "Best Radio Song" at the 2013 Shock Awards for "La Temperatura"
  • For two categories at the Billboard Music Awards, including Top Latin Artist.
  • In 2019 he was awarded El Premio ASCAP for Songwriter of the Year
  • He won a MTV Video Music Award for Best Latin this year, 2020
